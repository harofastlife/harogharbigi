About Haro Gharbigi

Haro Gharbigi is a Los Angeles-based entrepreneur, healthcare technology innovator, and software engineering leader with over 17 years of experience building and scaling technology businesses. As the Founder of AxiaASC, Haro is pioneering AI-powered software solutions for ambulatory surgery centers (ASCs), transforming how modern healthcare facilities operate.

Professional Background

Haro Gharbigi's career spans the full spectrum of technology leadership, from hands-on software engineering to executive-level business development. His journey includes founding multiple successful ventures, managing high-performing engineering teams, and driving digital transformation across industries including healthcare technology, affiliate marketing, and SaaS product development.

Current Ventures

Currently, Haro Gharbigi serves as the Founder of AxiaASC (October 2025 - Present), where he is building the next generation of ASC management software. AxiaASC combines artificial intelligence, real-time analytics, and automated scheduling to help ambulatory surgery centers improve operational efficiency and patient outcomes.

Previously, Haro co-founded Smash.com (January 2024 - December 2025), where he led product development and growth initiatives in the digital marketplace space.

Career Highlights

  • Affiliate Marketing Specialist at FASTLIFE LLC (April 2017 - April 2025): Led performance marketing strategies and optimization campaigns over an 8-year tenure, driving measurable growth and ROI.
  • Business Development Manager at EmailOversight (January 2023 - January 2024): Spearheaded business development initiatives, building strategic partnerships and expanding market reach in the email verification industry.
  • Senior Software Engineering Manager at iBrand LLC (July 2008 - April 2017): Managed software engineering teams for nearly a decade, overseeing architecture decisions, team development, and delivery of enterprise-grade software solutions.
