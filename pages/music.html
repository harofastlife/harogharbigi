Cultural Depth

Strong ties to heritage. Identity, memory, and continuity - grounded in origin while exploring everywhere else.

Musical Identity

What the music reveals about the listener:

Controlled Intensity

2Pac, Eminem, Wu-Tang Clan, GZA, Kanye West, TOOL, Black Sabbath, Pink Floyd - thoughtful aggression with philosophical edge. Music that pushes back, but only when it has something to say.

Era Collapse

Jumping effortlessly from 40s-60s jazz to 70s-80s soul, 90s-2000s alternative, and modern ambient. Confidence in taste - no need for music to validate, it validates itself.

Armenian Heritage

Harout Pamboukjian, Aram Asatryan, Martin Mkrtchyan, Arman Hovhannisyan, Element Band - not nostalgia for nostalgia's sake, but identity, memory, and continuity.

Atmosphere Curator

Sade, Portishead, Cigarettes After Sex, Bobby Caldwell, Simply Red, Ethereal in E - using music to regulate mental states: calm, late-night, reflective, sensual, focused.
